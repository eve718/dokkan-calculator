About the Dokkan Battle ATK Calculator

This free tool calculates exact ATK damage for Dokkan Battle events using event-specific formulas. Select an event, choose a stage and phase, enter your unit's ATK stat, and get accurate normal and super attack damage values instantly — no guesswork, no spreadsheets.

Currently supported events include Ultimate Red Zone editions (Pure Saiyans, DAIMA, Artificial Life Forms), Supreme Magnificent Battle (Next Generation Super Edition), Special Battle 2025, and The Greatest Tours. New events are added regularly after each global update.

All formulas are reverse-engineered from in-game data to match the exact DEF reduction, guard mechanics, and phase-specific modifiers each enemy applies. Use the results to plan your team's ATK investment and clear events reliably.
